About

I’m a developer passionate about crafting accessible, user-friendly web experiences that blend thoughtful design with solid engineering. My favorite work lies at the intersection of design and development, creating interfaces that not only look sharp but are thoughtfully built for performance and usability.

Currently, I’m a Web Developer at Industries Imaging and a Web Developer Intern at the City of Los Angeles Department of Recreation & Parks. I create and manage company and client sites in WordPress, build full Bootstrap-based websites for public programs (for example: SwimLA, PlayLA, and CLASS Parks) and real estate properties, and fix accessibility issues so these experiences remain fast, inclusive, and easy to navigate.

In the past, I studied Computer Science at Cal State LA, graduating on the Dean’s List, and have continued to grow through hands-on work with modern JavaScript, React, Node.js, and full-stack projects. I’ve shipped everything from smaller utilities to larger internal tools and hackathon apps, with a focus on clean, maintainable code and practical problem-solving.

In my spare time, I’m usually iterating on side projects like SparkTally, sharpening my skills through online courses, or jumping into a hackathon. Away from the screen, you can find me watching or playing sports, gaming, or hanging out with my chickens in the backyard.
